## üìä How Scoring Works

### Strategists (Max 100 pts + bonus):
- **Growth** (40 pts): Based on IG + Twitter followers, views/impressions, interactions
- **Creativity** (30 pts): Quality of content and creative ideas
- **Proactivity** (20 pts): Initiative and self-driven work
- **Leadership** (10 pts): Leading projects and guiding others

### Supports (Max 70 pts + bonus):
- **Growth** (20 pts): Half of strategist average (team contribution)
- **Creativity** (20 pts): Creative input and content support
- **Proactivity** (10 pts): Taking initiative
- **Collaboration** (20 pts): Teamwork and supporting strategists

### Bonus Points:
+5 points for every 10 followers contributed to Studio X brand accounts
